About

Introduction

Hi, my name is Dennis Loska and I am 24 years old. I want to become a developer to create awesome applications and websites to help others. I created this website to kick-start my career and to challenge myself. To me, the best way to learn is to gain experience by creating things. In order to accomplish this I will add references to my future projects on this site. I like teamwork and sharing ideas so I can learn from more experienced people and others from my humble knowledge. Since fall 2016 I am also a student of International Media and Computing at the HTW Berlin. So far I am very happy with this study field, because of the great equipment in the labs of the HTW and the hands-on mentality in classes. My favourite tools to work with so far are IntelliJ and SublimeText. Besides all this my favourite books to learn from are "Head First Java" by Kathy Sierra and Bert Bates and "HTML and CSS: Design and Build Websites" by Jon Duckett. I´m still not finished reading them, but I can highly recommend them to anyone who would like to learn Java and basic web-technologies.
